Welcome to Terraria Ranger Checklist!

This website will guide you through a typical Terraria 1.4 ranger playthrough

This is aimed entirely at a exclusively ranger playthrough. This site is heavily inspired by ewauq

Progress is not yet saved on your browser, do not close, otherwise progress will be lost.

If you want to delete your checklist data, click "reset" at top right of your screen.

Enjoy!

Legend

  • NPChow to unlock the character
  • EVENThow to trigger the event
  • BOSShow to summon the boss
  • MAIN WEAPONhow to get the main weapon
  • MAIN ARMORhow to get the main armor
Part One: Wood Tier
  • Chop trees down to build a house, get armor and wooden bow
  • Build a valid house for the Guide to move in
  • Gather ores
  • Craft a Gold/Platinum bow
  • Craft Golden/Platinum armor
  • Find Life Crystals in the caverns and only consume 4 of them
  • Use a Life Crystal to get the Nurse
  • The blood moon unlocks by also using a Life Crystal
  • Have 50 silver coins in your inventory to get the Merchant
  • Get an explosive item to get the Demolitionist
  • Find a dye plant to ge the dye trader
  • Stylist is found in the spiders nest
  • Angler is found in the ocean
  • Have a bullet or a gun to get the Arms Dealer
  • Get 53 bestiary entries to get the Zoologist
  • Defeat the King Slime using a Slime Crown
  • In the underground Desert mine fossil for the armor set
  • You can now get your health above 200 for the Eye of Cthulhu to spawn
  • Defeat the Eye of Cthulhu, can be spawned using the Suspicious Looking Eye
  • Make Demonite Armor
  • Make Demonite Bow
  • Dryad can move in after defeating the Eye of Cthulhu
